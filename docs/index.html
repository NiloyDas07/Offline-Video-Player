About This App

This offline video player allows you to watch videos directly from your local device. It supports custom subtitles, theme switching (light/dark), and can be installed as a Progressive Web App (PWA) for offline use, just like a native application.

Features:

  • Play local video files in various formats
  • Add and sync custom subtitle files (.srt, .vtt)
  • Light and dark theme support
  • Keyboard shortcuts for playback control
  • Installable as a PWA for offline use

How to Install & Use

Click on the following "Install" button and again click "Install" when the browser prompts you.

If the install button does not work as expected, you can try installing it manually by following the steps below:

  1. Install as an App (PWA)

    1. Open the App

      Visit this website in Chrome, Edge, or Brave on your computer or mobile device.

    2. Install the App

      On Desktop: Click the “Install” button in your browser’s address bar.
      On Android: Tap the three-dot menu and select “Add to Home screen” or “Install.”

    3. Launch from Home Screen

      Find the app icon on your home screen or app drawer and tap to open it.

    4. Enjoy Offline Playback

      Once installed, you can open and use the app without an internet connection.

    5. Play Your Videos

      Tap “Select Video” to choose a video file from your device. Use “Add Subtitles” to include subtitle files.

  2. Download the Complete Webpage

    1. Save the Page

      On Desktop: Right-click on the page and choose “Save As…”, then select “Webpage, Complete” and pick a folder.
      On Mobile: Open your browser’s menu and tap “Download page” or “Save page.”

    2. Open Locally

      Locate the downloaded HTML file and associated folder, then open the HTML in your browser to access the app offline.

    3. Play Your Videos

      Click “Select Video” within the locally opened page to choose and play video files from your device.
